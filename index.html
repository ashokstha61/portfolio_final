About me

I’m Ashok Kumar Shrestha, a dedicated software developer with a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science and Information Technology (BSc.CSIT) from Tribhuvan University. My passion for technology drives me to continuously expand my knowledge and skills, with a focus on mobile app development using Flutter and frontend development with ReactJS. I am proficient in several programming languages, including C/C++, Java, and Dart, and have hands-on experience working with databases like SQL and PostgreSQL.

Throughout my journey, I’ve had the opportunity to work on a variety of projects, from building expense trackers to quiz apps, weather forecast systems, and more. Each project has helped me refine my problem-solving abilities and grow as a developer. During my internship at Phoenix Solutions, I gained valuable experience in Java, working on ERP systems and developing solutions for government bodies.

I am passionate about crafting efficient, user-centric applications and enjoy staying up to date with the latest tech trends. I’m always looking for new challenges where I can apply my creativity, technical expertise, and teamwork to solve complex problems.
